A little about me

I am a software engineer passionate about creating technological solutions. In addition, I am a student of Geospatial Sciences, constantly seeking to expand my knowledge of spatial analysis, Geographic Information Systems (GIS), and how technology can be applied to better understand our world. Throughout my journey, I have sought to integrate my programming expertise with my passion for geospatial data, aiming to develop projects that provide new insights and help solve complex problems. I live in Brazil, a country with enormous potential for technological development, and I am always looking for new challenges that allow me to grow both professionally and personally. With a focus on innovation and continuous learning, my goal is to contribute to the advancement of technology and geospatial science by creating solutions that are not only efficient but also sustainable and accessible to everyone.
