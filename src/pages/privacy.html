Introduction


Welcome to Marco Aurélio's Portfolio!

Your Privacy is Very Important to Us. This Privacy Policy was created to inform you about how your personal information is collected, used, shared, and protected during your navigation and use of our website. This policy complies with the rules established by the General Data Protection Law (Law No. 13,709/2018 - LGPD) and the General Data Protection Regulation of the European Union (Regulation (EU) 2016/679 - GDPR), as well as other regulations applicable to personal data protection. By accessing or using the website, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, we recommend that you refrain from using the website. We believe in the importance of offering a rich, functional, and accessible experience for all users. By accessing or using this website, you agree to the terms described below, ensuring a safe and enjoyable browsing experience.

Definitions


To facilitate the understanding of this Privacy Policy, we have adopted the following definitions, as per the legal terms of the General Data Protection Law (LGPD) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):


  • "Personal Data": Any information related to an identified or identifiable natural person. This includes, but is not limited to, name, email address, phone number, physical address, location data, and other data that can directly identify an individual.

  • "Data Processing": Refers to any operation performed with Personal Data, from collection to disposal or anonymization. Data processing operations include, but are not limited to, collection, storage, organization, modification, consultation, use, sharing, transmission, deletion, or destruction of data.

  • "Data Controller": The person or entity responsible for determining the purposes and means of processing Personal Data. In the case of this website, the Data Controller is Marco Aurélio, who defines how and why your personal data is collected and processed.

  • "Data Processor": Entity or service provider that processes Personal Data on behalf of the Data Controller, according to their instructions and in compliance with applicable laws. The Data Processor may include companies that provide services such as hosting, data storage, marketing, among others, that process personal data according to the agreement made with the Data Controller.


What Personal Data Do We Collect?


Personal Data collection is done in a transparent and clear manner. Data may be collected directly from you or through automated technologies (such as cookies) when you interact with our website. Below, we list the types of data we may collect:


  • Identifiable Personal Data: They include information that can be used to directly identify a person, such as:

    • Full name

    • Email address

    • Phone number

    • Physical address

  • Browsing Data: We collect information about how you interact with our website, including:

    • IP address

    • Type of device and browser

    • Pages visited

    • Browsing time and interactions on the website

    • Approximate location (if permitted)

  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, personalize content, and analyze traffic. For more details about the cookies we use, please refer to our Cookie Policy.


    • How We Collect Your Personal Data?


    Personal data is collected in various ways, including but not limited to:



  • Forms on the website: For example, when filling out a contact form, subscribing to the newsletter, or making a service request.

  • Direct interactions: When you contact us via email, phone, or other communication channels.

  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use automated technologies, such as cookies, to collect browsing data while you interact with our website. You can choose to decline certain cookies or adjust your preferences in your browser at any time.


    • For What Purposes Do We Use Your Personal Data?


    The collection of personal data is carried out solely for the purposes described in this Policy. The processing of your data occurs based on the following purposes:



  • Provision of services and customer support: To respond to your service requests, customer support, technical assistance, and other services directly related to our portfolio.

  • Website improvements and personalization: We use your browsing data and preferences to improve the functionality and user experience on the website, including content personalization.

  • Sending communications: To send information related to new projects, updates, news, and personalized content, if you have subscribed to our newsletter or communication channels.

  • Compliance with legal obligations:To comply with legal, regulatory, or contractual obligations that apply to us.

    • Sharing of Personal Data.


    The personal data collected may be shared with third parties only in the following situations:



  • Service providers and partners: We may share your data with data processors, such as hosting providers, data analytics services, marketing and communication companies, among others, to perform operations essential for the functioning of our website and services, in compliance with data protection laws and ensuring that these third parties adhere to our data processing instructions.

  • As required by law: We may share your data with government authorities or third parties if required by law or regulation, such as in response to subpoenas or court orders.

  • International Data Transfers: If it is necessary to transfer your data outside of Brazil (or the European Union, for users from European countries), we will ensure that all appropriate legal measures are taken to ensure that the data is processed with an adequate level of protection.

    • Personal Data Security


    The security of your personal data is of utmost importance to us. We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, or destruction. This includes:



  • Encryption of sensitive data transmitted.

  • Restricted access roles to personal data within our organization.

  • Continuous monitoring of systems to identify vulnerabilities and prevent security incidents.

    • However, it is important to note that despite all efforts to protect your data, no system is 100% secure. Consequently, we cannot guarantee the absolute security of information transmitted over the Internet.



    Your Rights Regarding Personal Data


    You have the right at any time to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to the processing of your personal data, as established by the LGPD and GDPR. This includes the following rights:



  • Right of access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

  • Right to rectification: You can correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

  • Right to deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal data, unless there is a legal obligation or another legitimate reason to retain it.

  • Right to restriction of processing: You can request to restrict the processing of your data in certain circumstances, such as when the accuracy of the data is disputed.

  • Right to object: You can request the transfer of your personal data to another service provider, when applicable.

    • If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us as detailed in Section 10. How to Contact Us.



    Retention of Personal Data


    We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, or as required by legal or contractual obligations. When the data is no longer needed, we will take appropriate measures to securely delete it.
